AOISEIKOU
> what is aoiseikou
Aoiseikou is a record of what I see and experience in car culture. It’s a space to admire machinery on four wheels—from a Dacia Sandero to a Koenigsegg CC850.

Taste is subjective. I have my own, but it doesn’t dictate this space. This is a place to build, drive, share, and add to what already exists. Whatever you see here at the beginning — that’s just how car culture looks to me. You people add your own layers to it.
> origin
Founded by me and a close circle of enthusiasts, Aoiseikou started as a place to document car culture and save it in a way. What began as a small community gradually evolved into a broader project: documenting culture, building machines, and hosting events.
> pillars
machines — purpose-built cars and experimental builds
meetlog — documentation of meets and gatherings
culture — the philosophy, style, and attitude of the community
community — the people and projects behind Aoiseikou
> vision
Aoiseikou aims to grow into a larger automotive ecosystem: racing divisions, festivals, shows, and documentation that captures moments of car culture as they happen.
